Our Services

Psychoeducation and Mental Health Promotion

HOPMHEPS is the psychosocial and mental health promotion arm of Holiwells dedicated to promoting Mental health awareness, emotional intelligence and psychosocial support. At HOPMHEPS, we offer individual and group therapy, workshops, seminars, and programs designed to equip individuals, schools, and organizations with the skills and understanding needed to navigate mental health challenges effectively. Our approach blends scientific research with practical strategies, ensuring that participants gain actionable insights to improve their daily lives. Whether it's stress management, emotional regulation, or mental health literacy, HOCAMPS is committed to making psychological education accessible, engaging, and impactful. By bridging the gap between psychology and everyday life, we empower individuals to take charge of their mental well-being and foster healthier communities.

Substance Abuse Prevention & Intervention Consulting

HOSAPIS is the Substance Abuse Prevention & Intervention Consulting arm of Holiwells, dedicated to reducing substance abuse through awareness, intervention, and expert guidance. We believe that prevention, early support, and informed decision-making are key to fostering healthier communities. At HOSAPIS, we offer consulting services, intervention programs, and recovery support designed to help individuals, schools, and organizations address substance-related challenges. Our approach combines scientific research with practical strategies, ensuring that those affected receive effective, compassionate assistance tailored to their needs. Whether it’s risk assessment, addiction recovery, or policy development, HOSAPIS is committed to delivering impactful, evidence-based solutions. By bridging the gap between substance abuse education and real-world intervention, we empower individuals to break free from addiction and reclaim their lives.

Holiwells Life Coach Ministry

Life Coach Ministry is the personal development and transformational coaching arm of Holiwells, dedicated to guiding individuals toward self-discovery, purpose, and lasting success. We believe that with the right mindset and strategies, everyone can unlock their full potential. At Life Coach Ministry, we offer one-on-one coaching, group sessions, and self-improvement programs designed to help individuals, teams, and organizations navigate personal and professional growth. Our approach blends psychological insights with actionable techniques, ensuring that clients gain clarity, resilience, and motivation to achieve their goals. Whether it’s career advancement, emotional intelligence, or personal transformation, Life Coach Ministry is committed to making self-improvement accessible, engaging, and impactful. By bridging the gap between aspirations and tangible results, we empower individuals to create meaningful change.
